# MedVault

MedVault is a modern, comprehensive medical record and health management mobile application. It empowers users to take control of their healthcare journey by providing a secure, centralized hub for managing diagnoses, medications, and doctor appointments.

Designed with a focus on ease of use and visual excellence, MedVault ensures that critical health data is always at your fingertips, whether you're tracking a chronic condition or simply staying organized with routine checkups.
